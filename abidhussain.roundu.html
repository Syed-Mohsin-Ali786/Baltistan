ROUNDU

Roundu (or Rondu) Valley is a stunning, high-altitude district in
Gilgit-Baltistan, located roughly 65–70 km from Skardu along the Indus River and Karakoram Highway. Known for its
lush green meadows (Broq), dense pine forests, and towering mountain peaks, it acts as a, scenic,
less-crowded alternative to central Skardu, featuring breathtaking spots like Talu Broq and Tormik.
