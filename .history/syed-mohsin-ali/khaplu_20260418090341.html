Hushe Valley

This valley is renowned for its awe-inspiring mountains, including Masherbrum Peak, K-6 and K-7, and Laila Peak.

Chaqchan Mosque

Chaqchan Mosque is a top tourist attraction in Gilgit Baltistan, Khaplu. That captivates visitors with its serene beauty and historical significance. This ancient mosque, believed to be over 700 years old, holds a special place in the hearts of locals and tourists alike. Also with its traditional design and peaceful atmosphere. The mosque offers a tranquil space for spiritual reflection and exploration.

Khaplu Palace

The palace, considered an architectural heritage site and a significant tourist attraction,[2] was built in the mid-19th century to replace an earlier-dated fort located nearby. It served as a royal residence for the Raja of Khaplu.
